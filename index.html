Resume

I am a Full-Stack Web Developer based in Toronto. My journey here has been both nerve wrecking and exciting at the same time. It's that challenge that hooks me into every project I take on. I studied Full-Stack Web Development at Bitmaker-General Assemby Toronto. After working on a couple of projects my curiousty has steered me to enroll in a U/I & UX Design program during nights. Having knowledge of both front and back end development, gives me a different persceptive of the project from both sides. It is this knowledge that drives me to develop a keen eye for design while still thinking critical of the project implementation.

I try to keep it simple. My First thought is about can I drop? Then, how do I present it to the user? Can anything to hide? Finally, what can I displace? It is these refletions that guide my design decisions to a simple and thouhtful design. With the main focus on the main content, it helps deliver intended message effectively.

I am always open to consider other opinions and design ideas. This flexibility allows me to collaborate effectively. It is important to work together in order to yield a positive outcome that satisfies all stakeholders.
